PIPELINE

Five modules, one priority score

edi

Demand & gap scoring

Compares predicted vs. observed violations per grid-hour — or, in forecast mode, projects forward demand for hours that haven't happened yet.

flow_proxy

Obstruction proxy

Built from violation type and vehicle size — an explicitly labeled stand-in for real traffic-flow sensors, which this dataset doesn't have.

location_context

Junction matching

Keyword-matches junction_name strings against known landmarks. About 60% of zones have no named junction and stay UNCLASSIFIED.

optimizer

Officer allocation

Greedy allocation against a configurable officer budget, capped per physical location so coverage doesn't collapse onto a handful of hotspots.

repeat_offender

Vehicle risk tiers

Tracks repeat plates across the dataset window and tiers them LOW through CRITICAL based on violation frequency and concentration.

BEFORE YOU TRUST A NUMBER

What this is, and isn't

DATA WINDOW

Historical dataset capped at April 2024. Forecast mode projects the week right after that — a demonstration of the mechanism, not a live today-feed.

FLOW METRIC

No traffic sensors in this dataset. Flow impact is inferred from violation type and vehicle size, not measured.

SIMULATOR

The policy simulator re-runs the same scoring formula with different inputs. It shows how the recommendation shifts — not a causal forecast of real-world outcomes.
